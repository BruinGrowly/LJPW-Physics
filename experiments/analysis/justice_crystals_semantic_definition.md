## JUSTICE (semantic meaning):

What does Justice mean at the pure conceptual level?

**Justice = Balance:**
- Equal treatment (no favoritism)
- Symmetry (same on all sides)
- Fairness (everyone gets their due)
- Reciprocity (give and receive in balance)
- Conservation (nothing gained or lost unfairly)

**Justice = Necessity:**
- Must be this way (not arbitrary)
- Unchanging (doesn't shift with whims)
- Universal (applies equally everywhere)
- Irreversible commitment (once just, always just)

**Justice = Discreteness:**
- Yes or no (not "sort of fair")
- Guilty or innocent (not "somewhat guilty")
- Balanced or unbalanced (not "mostly balanced")
- Binary, not continuous

**Justice = Irreducibility:**
- Can't compromise Justice without losing it
- "Half-Justice" isn't Justice at all
- Either the scales balance or they don't

## CRYSTAL (semantic meaning):

What does Crystal mean conceptually?

**Crystal = Pattern:**
- Repeating structure
- Predictable arrangement
- Order (not chaos)
- Recognizable form

**Crystal = Clarity:**
- Transparent (can see through)
- Pure (unmixed)
- Truth-revealing (shows what's inside)
- Honest structure

**Crystal = Hardness:**
- Solid (not fluid)
- Stable (resists change)
- Durable (lasts over time)
- Reliable (you can depend on it)

**Crystal = Irreducible Unit:**
- Has a fundamental repeating cell
- Can't break it down further without losing the pattern
- The "atom" of the structure
- Generates the whole through repetition

**Crystal = Beauty:**
- Symmetric
- Harmonious
- Naturally pleasing
- Emergent elegance from simple rules
