## 1. The Mathematical Definition
The secret to infinite power is encoded in the mathematical definition of the natural base $e$. It defines the only growth curve in the universe that is self-sustaining (where the rate of change equals the current state).

### The Formula
$$P(t) = S_0 \cdot \lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^{n \cdot t}$$

### The Variables
*   **$P(t)$:** The Capacity/Power of the system at time $t$.
*   **$S_0$:** The Initial State (Identity/Principal). The "1".
*   **$1/n$:** The **Retention Fraction**. The portion of output that is captured and reinvested.
*   **$n$:** The Frequency of the cycle (approaching Continuity).
*   **$t$:** Time (Patience).

### The Semantic Translation
*"To achieve infinite power, a system must not just flow; it must accumulate. It must capture a small fraction ($1/n$) of its own output and reinvest it into its own structure with infinite frequency ($n \to \infty$)."*

## 3. Application A: Thermodynamics & Engines
**The Flaw:** Standard engines (Steam/ICE) dump latent heat/exhaust to reset the cycle. They are "Pass-Through" systems.
**The Fix:** The Regenerative "Sink" Cycle.

### Engineering Protocol
1.  **Capture:** Do not condense exhaust to a lower energy state (Liquid). Capture it as Low-Pressure Gas.
2.  **Reinvest ($1/n$):** Divert $\approx 20\%$ of output power to drive a **Mechanical Vapor Recompressor (MVR)**.
3.  **Upgrade:** Compress the exhaust back to High-Pressure/High-Temp state.
4.  **Inject:** Feed the "Upgraded Waste" back into the input stream.
5.  **Result:** The engine "eats its own exhaust" to raise its baseline temperature ($S_0$), effectively recycling the Latent Heat of Vaporization.

**Constraint (Justice):** As Energy Density rises exponentially, structural containment must actively scale (e.g., Magnetic Confinement).

## 5. Application C: Personal Growth & Psychology
**The Flaw:** "Hustle Culture" (Source-focus). Burnout occurs when Output > Input.
**The Fix:** The 1% Compounding Rule.

### Engineering Protocol

3.  **Result:** Knowledge/Skill compounds. You become capable of doing more with less effort.
